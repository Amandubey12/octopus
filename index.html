How it work

TechOctopus is a free-to-access digital library and content subscription service designed for global IT and modern technology professionals. The wide-ranging library of whitepapers, e-books, technical sheets, buyers guides, articles, and industry research covers your day-to-day information needs as today’s technology leader.

Our unique email service puts you in control of your content preferences, ensuring you always receive the most relevant content to support your current business or professional development requirements. It gives you control of what content you need and how frequently you want it.

All content is free to access and share, in exchange we ask that we have your permission to disclose your professional details with the authors or content providers.

You can find in the footer of this page links to jargon-free information on how we operate, and the data we process.

Want to spend less time looking?

As you’re probably already aware, at TechOctopus we’re here to help..
More specifically we’re here to save you time. If you’re bored of scrolling and searching through our
site then we can send you IT-related content straight to your inbox (don’t forget to check your junk).
Simply let us know what you’re interested in and we’ll make sure you’re the first person to know about it.
